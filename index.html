ABOUT US

Artificial Intelligence in Action is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a real impact on society and businesses through technology, particularly by leveraging artificial intelligence. Our mission is to facilitate the exchange of ideas, bring top experts together, organize events, and deliver technical projects that promote the use of artificial intelligence in addressing the real challenges faced by society and businesses. The AI in Action community of AI and machine learning experts, entrepreneurs, students, and programmers is a diverse and dynamic group of individuals eager to enhance their skills in machine learning and contribute to the advancement of technology for the common good. AI in Action is an innovative non-profit organization that provides unique opportunities for those interested in learning and contributing to the development of artificial intelligence for the benefit of society and businesses.

EVENTS BY ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE IN ACTION

AI and Machine Learning Workshop

The AI and Machine Learning Workshop is a monthly event designed for programmers with basic coding knowledge who are eager to expand their technical expertise in the fields of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Lasting between 3 and 5 hours, this hands-on workshop offers participants the opportunity to explore cutting-edge applications of AI across various industries, including:
- Spatial Data Processing: Leveraging AI for satellite imagery analysis and geospatial insights.
- Medicine: Enhancing healthcare through predictive analytics and diagnostic tools powered by machine learning.
- Agriculture: Optimizing crop management, pest control, and yield predictions with AI solutions.
- Sports: Analyzing performance metrics and strategies using AI-driven tools.
- Fake News Detection: Developing algorithms to identify and mitigate misinformation.
Each session is crafted to combine theoretical foundations with practical exercises, ensuring participants gain both conceptual understanding and hands-on experience. Whether you're looking to build your skills or explore the vast possibilities of AI, this workshop offers a supportive environment to learn, grow, and innovate. Empower your team to harness AI and machine learning for smarter strategies, sharper insights, and stronger results!

Meetups about Artificial Intelligence topics

AI and Machine learning Thematic Meetups are monthly events dedicated to the AI in Action community, entrepreneurs, and AI enthusiasts. These offline gatherings provide an excellent opportunity for learning, networking, and exchanging ideas. Each event features an invited speaker or expert who presents a use case, showcasing how artificial intelligence or machine learning is applied in a specific product, project, or industry. These meetups are designed as friendly events that encourage open dialogue and collaboration. Participants have the chance to discuss relevant topics, explore practical applications of AI and ML, and build valuable connections within a community passionate about technology and innovation. We encourage you to follow our social media pages to stay up to date with the date and time of the next event!

AI talks - meet the AI expert!

AI Talks is an online event series that brings together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence to explore practical use cases and share valuable insights. Each session features a local or international AI expert, who presents a real-world application of AI, offering participants a unique perspective on how these technologies are shaping industries and solving complex challenges. This event is designed to be interactive and informative, providing attendees with the opportunity to engage with the expert, ask questions, and gain a deeper understanding of AI's potential in various fields. Whether you’re a tech enthusiast, a professional, or simply curious about the world of AI, AI Talks is the perfect platform to stay informed, inspired, and connected to the global AI community. Join us online to explore how AI is transforming ideas into impactful solutions!

Artificial Intelligence Hackathons

A Hackathon is an intensive and collaborative event where participants come together to solve real-world challenges using artificial intelligence and machine learning. The hackathon spans 3 days and brings together 70-80 participants, including programmers, data scientists, designers, and entrepreneurs. The event is structured to foster innovation and teamwork, with the support of product mentors and technical experts, who guide the teams throughout the process. Participants work in small groups to generate ideas, develop prototypes, and present AI-based solutions to specific problems, often focusing on areas such as healthcare, agriculture, space data, or fake news detection. AI Hackathons are not just about coding—they are about collaboration, creativity, and turning ideas into impactful solutions. It is an unforgettable experience for anyone passionate about technology and problem-solving!

PROJECTS CARRIED OUT DURING EVENTS

În cadrul evenimentelor organizate de Inteligența Artificială în Acțiune, participanții și membrii comunității au folosit diverse tehnologii pentru a dezvolta soluții inovatoare. Iată proiectele și tehnologiile utilizate pentru fiecare:

  • Tree Counting - Environment and Forestry - Computer Vision for analyzing drone-captured images.
    - Deep Learning algorithms for automatically detecting and counting trees.
    - Integration with GIS (Geographic Information Systems) for mapping and monitoring.
  • Chatbot for Real Estate - Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand and respond to user inquiries.
    - Chatbot development platforms such as Dialogflow or Rasa.
    - Integration with databases to provide information about construction phases and design options.
  • Waste Detection - Heavy Industry - Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for classifying waste images into categories such as paper, plastic, organic, and metal.
    - Image Segmentation algorithms to separate objects in complex images.
    - Computer Vision for analyzing images captured by video cameras in automated sorting processes.
  • Fruit Detection - Agriculture - Machine Learning for video analysis and estimating fruit quantities.
    - Computer Vision for automatically detecting and counting fruits in images.
    - Image processing algorithms like YOLO (You Only Look Once) for fast and accurate fruit identification.
  • Media Article Classification - Media Industry - Natural Language Processing (NLP) for text analysis in articles.
    - Sentiment Analysis and Text Classification algorithms to detect manipulative language and verify the right of reply.
    - Web Scraping technologies for collecting data from online articles.
  • What’s in This Screenshot? - Marketing and Real Estate - Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for extracting text from screenshots.
    - Chatbot API for quick user interaction.
    - Image Classification algorithms to identify visual content and link it to company details.
